              Welcome To FSSM-GA

              Name

               Introduction To Applied Geophysics STU S3/S5

               

              Description

              An introduction to using physical principles to explore the Earths subsurface, with an emphasis on near-surface applications. Topics will include seismic, gravity, magnetic, electrical, and electromagnetic surveying techniques, and their application in prospecting, hydrogeology, environmental assessments, and well logging.
